// About

Professional drummer and percussionist based in the UK, specialising in jazz, session, and theatre work.

Josh Savage is a Manchester-based musician working across performance, recording, music direction, and production. Since graduating from the Royal Northern College of Music, variety has been central to his work — collaborating with artists across genres, teaching a small group of private students, and directing select amateur ensembles.

Alongside performing, Josh is Co-Director of A&J Bespoke Music House and also works as a producer with a small roster of artists.

Josh regularly performs in Manchester at venues such as Albert Schloss, Blues Kitchen Manchester, Band on the Wall, and Matt and Phreds.
