// Teaching Philosophy

Let the music inform our learning.

Our first lesson will involve talking about your musical influences, listening to music, and discussing what's going on behind the scenes that makes that music really work.

Then begins the playing! We'll work on musically informed grooves, fills, and the techniques we need to support them. We'll also challenge ourselves with co-ordination practice linked to the tunes, and some goal setting to ensure we stay on track from week to week.

// Lesson Types

Private 1-to-1 Lessons

Personalised lessons tailored to your goals, skill level, and musical interests. Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine advanced techniques.

  • Customised lesson plans
  • Flexible scheduling
  • All skill levels welcome
  • Focus on your goals

Group Workshops

Collaborative learning sessions on different topics for small groups (2-3). Great for building ensemble skills, learning from peers, and developing musicianship in a social setting.

  • Small group sizes
  • Ensemble playing
  • Peer learning

Band Coaching

Full band sessions to help improve tightness, dynamics, and overall level of performance. Ideal for bands preparing for gigs, recordings, or looking for fresh ears to hear their material.

  • Full band sessions
  • Arrangement advice
  • Performance coaching
  • Recording preparation
